# The Standard Model of Particle Physics

The Standard Model classifies all known elementary particles and describes three of the four fundamental forces (electromagnetism, weak interaction, and strong interaction).

## Core Particle Categories

1. **Matter Particles (Fermions)**: Spin-1/2 particles that obey Fermi-Dirac statistics.
   - Learn more in [Fermions](fermions.md).
2. **Force Carriers (Bosons)**: Spin-1 particles that mediate fundamental forces.
   - Learn more in [Bosons](bosons.md).
3. **Mass Generation (Higgs Boson)**: The spin-0 scalar particle.
   - Learn more in [Higgs Boson](higgs.md).

Fundamental interactions are explained by gauge field theories.
