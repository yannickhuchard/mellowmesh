# Gauge Bosons

Bosons are particles with integer spin that mediate the fundamental forces of nature according to gauge theories.

## The Force Carriers

1. **Photon** ($\gamma$): Mediates electromagnetism. Massless, infinite range.
2. **Gluon** ($g$): Mediates the strong interaction between [Quarks](quarks.md). Massless, but color confinement limits range.
3. **W and Z Bosons** ($W^\pm, Z^0$): Mediate the weak interaction responsible for radioactive decay. Very massive, short range.

All massive particles acquire mass via coupling to the scalar [Higgs Boson](higgs.md).
