# Fermions

Fermions are the building blocks of matter. They have half-integer spin and obey the Pauli Exclusion Principle.

## Sub-categories

Fermions are split into two major groups, each consisting of three generations:

1. **Quarks**: Particles that feel the strong force and make up hadrons (like protons and neutrons).
   - Learn more in [Quarks](quarks.md).
2. **Leptons**: Particles that do not feel the strong force.
   - Learn more in [Leptons](leptons.md).
