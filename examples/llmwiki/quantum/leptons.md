# Leptons

Leptons are elementary fermions that do not undergo strong interactions.

## Classification

Leptons are split into charged leptons and neutral leptons (neutrinos):

1. **Charged Leptons**:
   - **Electron** ($e$): Responsible for electricity and chemistry.
   - **Muon** ($\mu$): Heavier cousin of the electron, unstable.
   - **Tau** ($\tau$): Very heavy, extremely short-lived.
2. **Neutral Leptons (Neutrinos)**:
   - **Electron Neutrino** ($\nu_e$)
   - **Muon Neutrino** ($\nu_\mu$)
   - **Tau Neutrino** ($\nu_\tau$)

Neutrinos only interact via gravity and the weak force, mediated by W/Z [Bosons](bosons.md).
