# Quarks

Quarks are elementary fermions characterized by color charge, fractional electric charge, and weak isospin. They are never found in isolation due to color confinement.

## Flavors

There are six quark flavors organized in three generations:
1. **First Generation**: Up ($u$) and Down ($d$) quarks. These form protons ($uud$) and neutrons ($udd$).
2. **Second Generation**: Charm ($c$) and Strange ($s$) quarks.
3. **Third Generation**: Top ($t$) and Bottom ($b$) quarks. The top quark is the most massive elementary particle.

Quarks interact by exchanging gluons, which are a type of [Boson](bosons.md).
