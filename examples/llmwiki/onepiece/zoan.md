# Zoan Devil Fruits

Zoan fruits allow the user to transform into another species, dramatically boosting physical strength and close-quarters combat capabilities.

## Sub-types

1. **Normal Zoan**: Normal animals (e.g. Model: Falcon, Model: Leopard).
2. **Ancient Zoan**: Extinct creatures like dinosaurs (e.g. Model: Allosaurus, Model: Triceratops).
3. **Mythical Zoan**: Legendary mythological creatures (e.g. Model: Phoenix, Model: Azure Dragon). These are extremely rare and grant additional unique powers.

The most famous Mythical Zoan is the [Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika](nika.md), which was historically hidden under the name of a [Paramecia](paramecia.md) fruit.
