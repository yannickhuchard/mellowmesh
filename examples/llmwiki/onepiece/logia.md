# Logia Devil Fruits

Logia fruits are considered the most powerful of the three classes. They allow the user to generate, control, and transform their entire body into a natural element.

## Prominent Examples

*   **Mera Mera no Mi** (Flame-Flame Fruit): Control and transform into fire. Consumed by Portgas D. Ace, later by Sabo.
*   **Goro Goro no Mi** (Rumble-Rumble Fruit): Control and transform into electricity. Consumed by Enel.
*   **Hie Hie no Mi** (Ice-Ice Fruit): Control and transform into ice. Consumed by Kuzan (Aokiji).
*   **Yami Yami no Mi** (Dark-Dark Fruit): Control darkness and gravity. Consumed by Blackbeard. Unlike other Logias, it does not allow the user to become intangible, but it can nullify other Devil Fruit powers.

Logia powers are often compared to the legendary Mythical Zoan forms (see [Zoan](zoan.md) and [Model: Nika](nika.md)).
