# Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika

The **Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika** (Human-Human Fruit, Model: Nika) is a Mythical Zoan-class Devil Fruit that allows the user to transform into the legendary "Sun God" Nika, gaining a rubber-like body and the freedom to fight in any way they fancy, bringing smiles to people.

## Historical Concealment

The World Government renamed this fruit the **Gomu Gomu no Mi** (Rubber-Rubber Fruit) and classified it as a [Paramecia](paramecia.md) fruit to hide its existence from history.

*   **User**: Monkey D. Luffy.
*   **Awakening (Gear 5)**: When awakened, the user's rubber body becomes even stronger, allowing them to affect the environment (turning ground into rubber) and fight with complete cartoonish freedom.

This fruit bridges the [Zoan](zoan.md) and [Paramecia](paramecia.md) classes due to its dual nature of body mutation and full creature transformation.
