# Paramecia Devil Fruits

Paramecia fruits are the most common class, granting users a massive variety of superhuman abilities that don't fit into Logia or Zoan classifications.

## Prominent Examples

*   **Gomu Gomu no Mi** (Rubber-Rubber Fruit): Grants a body made of rubber. Used by Luffy. (Later revealed to have a hidden identity; see [Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika](nika.md)).
*   **Gura Gura no Mi** (Tremor-Tremor Fruit): Allows the creation of powerful shockwaves and earthquakes. Consumed by Whitebeard, later stolen by Blackbeard (who also holds a [Logia](logia.md) fruit).
*   **Ope Ope no Mi** (Op-Op Fruit): Allows the user to create a spherical space ("Room") where they can manipulate anything (surgery, teleportation, heart extraction). Consumed by Trafalgar Law.
